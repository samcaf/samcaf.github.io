About Me

Hi, I'm Sam! I'm a PhD student in Nuclear and Particle Theory in the Center for Theoretical Physics at MIT. I'm interested in the foundations of quantum field theory (QFT), functional techniques in QFT, strongly coupled gauge theories -- including QCD phenomenology and jets -- quantum chaos, and thermalization. I'm located in Cambridge, MA.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, climbing, and playing chess.
